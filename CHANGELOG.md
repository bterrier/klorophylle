## [Unreleased]

### Added
- Initial public release of Klorophylle: a plant-first, offline single-device desktop product
  (Linux). Plant-first UI, plant catalog with care thresholds, care status (incl. light as DLI),
  care journal with photo attachments, BLE scanning + live values + GATT history backfill, bucketed
  reading history, QtGraphs charts, settings/units, care notifications, export/backup, a
  WatchFlower `data.db` importer, and an embedded local-first AI care assistant.
